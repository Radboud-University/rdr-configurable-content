Welcome to the Donders Repository

The Donders Institute for Brain, Cognition and Behaviour is a world-leading research centre devoted to understanding the mechanistic underpinnings of human cognition and behavior in health and disease. The Institute is home to more than 600 researchers from 35 countries who share the common goal of contributing to the advancement of the brain-, cognitive- and behavioral sciences through investigator-driven research, and improving health, education and technology by applying advances in this field.

This repository is being used by 522 researchers to manage their research data, organized in 315 work-in-progress and 7 finalized data acquisition collections, 194 work-in-progress and 4 finalized research documentation collections and 53 work-in-progress and 27 published data sharing collections. Everyone can request access to the published data sharing collections.

Please contact your data steward for instructions how to archive and/or share your research data.
